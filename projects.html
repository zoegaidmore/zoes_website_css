Happiness Index versus Urbanization Data Analysis

For COGS 108, a data science class at UCSD, I worked in a group to do research on happiness: what factors lead to higher happiness in a country? The key factor that we wanted to analyze was urbanization, as we hypothesized that a higher proportion of a country living in an urban area would lead to higher happiness in that country. However, through our analysis, we found that that isn't always the case. We discovered that many factors, like a country's GDP, social support, life expectancy, and urbanization all impacted a country's happiness.
