About Me

All things Zoe!

I'm Zoe Gaidmore, a second year at the University of California, San Diego. I'm majoring in computer science, and minoring in business. After I graduate, I hope to work in software development. I am also passionate about helping others learn about computer science. I started a club at an elementary school in my hometown that was dedicated to helping elementary school students learn about coding. I also worked at a summer school which aimed to provide supplemental education to low-income children in my community, in math, science, and coding. Lastly, I am an Instructional Assistant here at UCSD, and am the director of Academics for Pi Beta Phi.

Outside of school, I enjoy various hobbies. I like to work out, whether it's a cycling class, hot yoga, or hike. I also enjoy reading, baking, and watching reality TV. I have golfed and Irish danced all my life. Overall, I love spending my free time with my friends, family, and Basset Hound Bella.
